The most powerful ideas, companies, and industries don't appear overnight.

During the next 18 months, Europe will become home to one of the world's biggest regulated markets for crypto assets through the MiCA Directive. In what ways will it affect the industry?

The European WEB3.0 Summit 2023 brings together European state officials, business leaders, media, and civil society to foster a robust and sustainable environment for WEB3.0 in Europe.

FORMAT

European Web3.0 Summit 2023 is an invitation-only event for 500 guests that brings together European state officials, business leaders, and civil society to foster a robust, sustainable, and rapidly developing environment for Web3.0 companies and communities.
