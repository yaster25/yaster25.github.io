This unique event will take place in an equally unique place: a nineteenth-century neo-Gothic jewel, Antwerp Trade Fair. As in the 16th century, the Handelsbeurs is an inspiring meeting place and economic attraction. During Antwerp's Golden Age, this “mother of all fairs” was the model for many other fairs in Europe.

The Handelsbeurs, which was reconstructed in 1872 after a fire, has served for centuries as a busy trading place and court hall. Even the Royal Exchange in London has been inspired by the Antwerp example. From 2003, onwards the impressive building was vacant for quite some time, until October 2019, when the breathtaking Stock Exchange opened its doors again, after a thorough renovation.

Now the Handelsbeurs is perfect for large conferences, parties, events and presentations – with spacious multifunctional meeting rooms and break-out rooms with decorative stained-glass windows, beautiful parquet floors and high ceilings.

There are several luxury hotels near the summit venue: Saphire House Antwerp, Handelsbeurs Antwerpen, and Hilton Antwerp Old Town.
